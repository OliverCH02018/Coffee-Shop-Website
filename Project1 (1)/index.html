ABOUT US

Prep's Coffee Shop was founded in 2018 by the Smith family, who have a passion for coffee and a love for their community. We use only the finest beans, sourced from local roasters, to create a unique and rich flavor in every cup.

We are committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly materials, and supporting local charities and initiatives. Come and join us in making a difference, one cup of coffee at a time.
